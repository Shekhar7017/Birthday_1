RISHAV KUMAR BIRTHDAY WEBSITE - PHOTO SETUP

Place these seven photos inside the "assets" folder:

1. rishav-hero.jpg  - Main hero photo (landscape photo works best)
2. rishav-1.jpg     - Gallery photo 1
3. rishav-2.jpg     - Gallery photo 2
4. rishav-3.jpg     - Gallery photo 3
5. rishav-4.jpg     - Gallery photo 4
6. rishav-5.jpg     - Gallery photo 5
7. rishav-6.jpg     - Gallery photo 6

Recommended:
- Hero: landscape image, at least 1600 x 900 pixels
- Gallery: clear JPG photos, at least 1000 pixels on the longest side
- Keep the filenames exactly as listed above

Hero face position:
- The layout keeps the top of portrait photos visible so the face is not cut.
- If a future photo needs adjustment, open styles.css and change --hero-focus-y.
- Smaller percentage moves the visible crop upward; larger percentage moves it down.

Until these photos are added, the website automatically uses the included
cinematic birthday artwork as a fallback.

The website also includes animated balloons, confetti, birthday symbols,
an opening celebration burst, and a larger celebration after the candle wish.

Open index.html to view the website.
